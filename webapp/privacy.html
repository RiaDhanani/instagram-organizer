Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The short version: We collect nothing. All your data stays in your browser. The only external service this app contacts is OpenAI — directly from your browser, using your own API key.

What we collect

Nothing. Saved Posts Organizer does not have a backend server, does not set cookies for tracking, and does not collect analytics or personal data of any kind.

Where your data lives

All data is stored locally in your browser:

  • Saved post data (URLs, thumbnail URLs, alt text) is stored in your browser's localStorage.
  • Your OpenAI API key is stored in localStorage (webapp) or chrome.storage.local (extension). It never leaves your device except to call OpenAI's API directly.
  • If you use the Chrome extension, scraped post data is temporarily held in chrome.storage.local to transfer it to the organizer tab, then immediately cleared.

What gets sent to OpenAI

When you click "Categorize", the following is sent to OpenAI's API using your own API key:

  • Thumbnail image URLs (fetched directly from Instagram's CDN by OpenAI)
  • Alt text descriptions scraped from Instagram posts

This data is sent to categorize your posts. Your use of the OpenAI API is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Instagram data

The Chrome extension scrapes only publicly accessible data from your own saved posts page (post URLs, thumbnail URLs, and alt text). No passwords, tokens, or private account data are accessed or stored.

Third-party services

The only third-party service contacted is OpenAI (api.openai.com), and only when you initiate categorization. No other third parties receive your data.

Data deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by clicking Settings → Clear All Data in the app. This removes all post data and your API key from localStorage.
